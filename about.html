Who We Are

Built on a simple but powerful idea.

Noe Builders was born from a simple but powerful inspiration: to build in the most natural way possible. We are driven by a passion for natural materials and healthy living environments — creating spaces that heal, inspire, and endure.

Our mission is to create the healthiest buildings in harmony with nature, inspiring new standards in natural architecture and ecological building. Every decision, from structure to interior finish, reflects a commitment to health, sustainability, and beautiful craftsmanship.

Through our alliance with COCO-MAT and selected partners, we offer fully integrated solutions — from design through supply, construction, and interior finishing. We are not just builders. We are custodians of a better way to build.

What We Believe

Our values

01

Health First

Every material, every decision starts with one question: is this good for the people who will live and work here? We build for human health, not convenience.

02

Nature in Harmony

We don't build against nature — we build with it. Our structures are designed to work with natural systems, not override them.

03

Honest Craft

We believe in the beauty of honest materials — things that show their age with grace, that carry the marks of the hands that made them.
