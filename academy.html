About the Academy

Where knowledge meets nature.

Noe Academy is the educational arm of NOE Builders — a platform dedicated to sharing knowledge, building community, and inspiring the next generation of natural builders.

Led by Dylan, our event planning specialist, the Academy brings together craftspeople, architects, clients, and nature lovers to explore the world of natural building through workshops, talks, site visits, and community events.

The Academy is currently in development. Sign up below to be the first to hear about upcoming events and programmes.

🎓

Workshops

Hands-on workshops in natural building techniques — from cob and lime plastering to wood joinery and wool insulation. Learn by doing, guided by experts.

Talks & Events
