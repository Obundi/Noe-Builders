Our Services

From first concept to final finish — we cover every dimension of natural building.

First Pillar

Creation

Every great building begins as an idea. Our creation process is deeply collaborative — we work alongside you, architects, craftspeople, and specialists to shape a vision that is true to you and true to nature.

Concept Developing

Collaborative process; every project starts with a clear idea and grows through working closely with clients, makers, and specialists. We believe the best outcomes emerge when all voices are heard from the start.

Our Namesake, Our Soul

Designing

Design is at the core of everything we do — natural materials shaped with care and intention. We create spaces that feel inevitable, as though they could not have been built any other way.

Tear-Shaped Gifts

Architecture

Collaborating with like-minded architects, we create spaces that are functional and deeply connected to their surroundings. Architecture as a gift to the land it stands on.

02
Second Pillar

Building

The craft of construction — honest, skilled, and rooted in natural methods. We build with materials that breathe, age gracefully, and leave the lightest possible footprint on the earth.

Suriname · Construction
Undisputed King

Wood Construction

Renewable, strong, alive. Carefully selected timber using refined methods for durable, circular structures. Wood is not just a material — it is a living testament to the forest it came from.

All Purpose

Wool Insulation

A natural extension of the building philosophy — wool supports a healthy indoor climate by regulating humidity, absorbing sound, and keeping spaces warm without synthetic chemicals.

Nature Lover

Permaculture

Integrating natural systems directly into the built environment. We design with the land, not against it — creating spaces where building and nature exist in genuine harmony.

03
Third Pillar

Products

Supply and finishing — every material chosen for health, beauty, and longevity. From the floor beneath your feet to the furniture you rest on, every product we supply is natural, non-toxic, and built to last.

Indonesia · Timber yard
Precious Mane

Flooring

Natural wood flooring with breathable non-toxic oil finish — it ages beautifully, developing character over time. Each plank tells the story of the tree it came from.

Unexpected Beauties

Furniture

Minimalist solid wood collections — timeless, elegant, made to last a lifetime. Our furniture is designed to be passed down, not replaced. Honest craft, enduring beauty.

Colourful

Tiles

Hand-shaped tiles with natural imperfections — mineral-based, durable, and deeply tactile. No two tiles are identical. Each one carries the mark of the hands that made it.
