Who We Are

More than a builder.
A philosophy.

Noe Builders was born from a simple but powerful inspiration: to build in the most natural way possible. We are driven by a passion for natural materials and healthy living environments — creating spaces that heal, inspire, and endure.

"You step into a space. A wooden home — warm, calm, pure. Everything you see, from the floor to the bed, from the insulation to the shampoo, is natural. And everything is designed, built, or supplied by Noe Builders."
— The NOE Vision

Our Mission

The healthiest buildings in harmony with nature.

Our mission is to create living environments that are good for people and good for the planet. We believe the materials around us profoundly affect our health, mood, and wellbeing — and we build accordingly.

Through our alliance with COCO-MAT and selected partners, we offer fully integrated solutions — from design through supply, construction, and interior finishing.
