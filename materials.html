What We Build With

Natural Materials

Every material we use is chosen for its health benefits, environmental integrity, and enduring beauty.

Our Materials

Built from the earth,
returned to nature.

We build exclusively with materials that breathe, age gracefully, and contribute to the health of the people who live and work within them.

🌲

Wood

The undisputed king of natural building materials. Carefully selected timber reduces stress, lowers heart rates, and creates a deep biophilic connection to nature.

Stress reduction · Acoustic benefits · Biophilic connection
🌿

Hemp

Non-toxic, breathable, and anti-fungal. Hemp insulation and construction actively improves indoor air quality while sequestering carbon throughout its lifetime.

Non-toxic · Breathable · Anti-fungal · Air quality
🐑

Wool

Nature's perfect insulator. Wool absorbs odours and sound, regulates humidity naturally, and keeps spaces warm in winter and cool in summer — without chemicals.

Humidity regulation · Sound absorption · Odour control
🔶

Copper

Anti-microbial and timeless. Copper brings vitality to a space and develops a beautiful patina over time — a living reminder of temporality and honest craftsmanship.

Anti-microbial · Timeless · Living finish
🪨

Chalk / Lime

High pH and naturally breathable, lime creates a living environment. Flexible and self-healing, lime plaster has been used for thousands of years for good reason.

Breathable · Flexible · Natural living environment
🏺

Cob

Earth, sand, and straw combined into one of the oldest building materials on earth. Naturally breathing and thermally comfortable — cob creates beautifully warm, durable walls.

Thermal mass · Breathing walls · Thermal comfort
🎋

Bamboo

One of the fastest-growing plants on earth, bamboo is renewable, strong, and beautiful. It creates a biophilic connection while leaving a minimal environmental footprint.

Rapid regeneration · Strong · Biophilic
🌍

Earth

Rammed earth, adobe, and earthen plasters — building with the ground itself. Earth construction is amongst the most thermally efficient and naturally beautiful methods known.

Thermal mass · Zero waste · Timeless
🪨

Stone

Enduring, honest, and beautiful. Stone carries the history of the landscape it comes from, creating spaces with a sense of permanence and deep connection to place.

Durability · Thermal mass · Timeless
